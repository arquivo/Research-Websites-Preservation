          <div class="slogan">
<h3>Welcome to ORFEUS Project</h3>
</div>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter" alt="slhome01b" src="http://orfeus.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/12/slhome01b.jpg" /></p>
<div class="spacer" style="height:px;"></div>
<div class="col_23">
<h4>What is ORFEUS project</h4>
<p>ORFEUS is a full scale EU-financed demonstration project aiming at progressing the previously developed Horizontal Directional Drilling drill tip Radar that was proven under the preceding project entitled “<a href="http://www.orfeus-project.eu/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.orfeus-project.eu']);" target="_blank">Optimised Radar to Find Every Utility in the Street</a>” to a commercial stage.</p>
<div class="spacer" style="height:px;"></div>
<div class="heading-block"><span>What we do</span></div><div class="col_13">
<h4>Research</h4>
<p>Technical refinement, laboratory and field testing, technical specification and prototype system validation</p>
</div>
<div class="col_13 last">
<h4 class="title">Demonstration</h4>
<p>Demonstrate a unique, high performance, commercially viable product that will increase significantly the HDD use</p>
</div>
<div class="col_13">
<h4 class="title">Promotion &amp; Networking</h4>
<p>Technical conferences, workshops &amp; co-operation with utilities, HDD industry, standardisation bodies, research organisations, other end users</p>
</div>
<div class="col_13 last">
<h4>Exploitation &amp; Sales</h4>
<p>The consortium published a questionnaire seeking the views of interested parties on the ORFEUS technology. You are kindly invited to <a href="http://goo.gl/forms/t0QgEIHlUB" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://goo.gl']);">fill it in</a>.</p>
</div>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<div class="spacer" style="height:px;"></div>
<div class="heading-block"><span>Our Publications</span></div><div class="col_13">
<h4>Technical Papers</h4>
<p>Interim research and demonstration findings will be presented at technical conferences devoted to no-dig technologies and microwave engineering and will be published in journals.</p>
</div>
<div class="col_13 last">
<h4>Tutorials and prototype demonstrations</h4>
<p>Video clips to demonstrate the drill tip radar system in field conditions as well as multimedia information on project results.</p>
</div>
<div class="col_13">
<h4>Newsletters</h4>
<p>Publication of regular newsletters and project related dissemination material.
</p></div>
