<p><strong><span style="font-family: mceinline;">This project was aimed at designing and&nbsp;developing a "HEELLESS" running shoe and to investigate the effect of&nbsp;such a shoe on running. This project is now concluded.&nbsp;</span></strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><strong><span style="font-size: 14.0pt; line-height: 115%; font-family: "><span style="font-family: mceinline;">Concluding remarks&nbsp;</span></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm; text-align: justify;"><strong><span style="font-family: "><span style="font-family: mceinline;">Introduction: </span></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: "><span style="font-family: mceinline;">Athletes whilst running generally land their foot, not with the heel, but on the ball of the foot or in between the heel and the forefoot. &nbsp;On the other hand, people who jog tend to strike their heels on the ground. The innovative shoe designs were developed to assist people in their fitness training and prevent overuse injuries forces during running. </span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: mceinline;"><span style="font-family: ">The heelless running shoe is an innovative concept which aims to reduce the repetitive strain from high heel impact during running. (for more details please see the patent document in the following link:&nbsp; </span><a href="http://www.google.com/patents/US8387285"><span style="font-family: ">http://www.google.com/patents/US8387285</span></a><span style="font-family: ">)</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm; text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: "><span style="font-family: mceinline;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm;"><strong><span style="font-family: "><span style="font-family: mceinline;">Publicity: </span></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: mceinline;"><span style="font-family: ">Project HEELLESS was commended by the external reviewers and was included in Horizon 2020, </span><span style="font-family: ">The EU Framework Programme for Research and Innovation: </span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: mceinline;"><a href="http://ec.europa.eu/small-business/success-stories/2012/february/index_en.htm"><span style="font-family: ">http://ec.europa.eu/small-business/success-stories/2012/february/index_en.htm</span></a></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: mceinline;">Project HEELLESS was regarded as a success story in the European Small Business Portal.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: mceinline;"><a href="http://ec.europa.eu/programmes/horizon2020/en/news/new-running-shoe-helps-prevent-injury"><span style="font-family: ">http://ec.europa.eu/programmes/horizon2020/en/news/new-running-shoe-helps-prevent-injury</span></a><span class="MsoHyperlink"></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm;"><strong><span style="font-family: "><span style="font-family: mceinline;">What next: </span></span></strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: "><span style="font-family: mceinline;">Project HEELLESS had stemmed from earlier research collaboration between Healus Technology and the Clinical Biomechanics Research Team at Staffordshire University (2004-2008), and after completion of this project, this collaboration has continued. </span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: mceinline;">Further research is currently underway to optimise the composite formula of the mid and outer sole, which are the sole parts below the fully developed natural fibre-reinforced sole plate.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 5pt 0cm 5pt 36pt;"><span style="font-family: mceinline;"><span style="font-family: ">Talks are taking place between </span><span style="font-family: ">Healus Ltd </span><span style="font-family: ">and footwear brand manufacturers to produce the footwear at large quantities and bring it to market.&nbsp;</span></span></p>                    </div>
