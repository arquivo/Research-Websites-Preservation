<p style="text-align: left;"><strong>AEGOS is a support action (December 2008 - November 2011) of the European Union 7<sup>th</sup> Research & Technological Development Framework.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong><br /></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><img width="13" height="10" src="/images/stories/from_brgm/Resized/puce_13x10.gif" alt="alt" /> <strong>Design</strong> a pan-African infrastructure of interoperable data and user-oriented services to strengthen the sustainable use of georesources in Africa.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><img width="13" height="10" src="/images/stories/from_brgm/Resized/puce_13x10.gif" alt="alt" /><strong> </strong><strong>Contribute</strong> to the Global Earth Observation System of Systems (GEOSS) through the  setting-up of a Solid Earth observation system in Africa.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><img width="13" height="10" src="/images/stories/from_brgm/Resized/puce_13x10.gif" alt="alt" /> <strong>Elaborate </strong>common strategies for <strong>capacity building </strong>and <strong>training programmes.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><strong> </strong><img width="13" height="10" src="/images/stories/from_brgm/Resized/puce_13x10.gif" alt="alt" /><strong> </strong><strong>Support geoscientific communities </strong>and institutional decision-makers for setting up sustainable development research and public policies.</p>
