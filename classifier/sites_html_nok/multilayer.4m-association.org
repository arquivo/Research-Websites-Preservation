  <div class="meta">
  
  </div>

  <div class="content">
    <p><strong>PhD Research Studentship</strong></p>

<p>Development of optical coordinate metrology techniques for precision and additive manufactured components
Applications are invited from suitably qualified graduates for a fully funded PhD studentship within the Manufacturing Metrology Group. The Faculty is ranked in the top 5 out of 85 engineering departments in the UK in the last Research Assessment Exercise (RAE 2008), with most of its research output classified as world leading or internationally excellent in quality.</p>

    <h2>4M/ICOMM 2015 – Call for Papers</h2>
