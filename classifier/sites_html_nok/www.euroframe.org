      <h4>European Forecasting Research Association for the Macro-Economy</h4>
<h1>The EUROFRAME Network</h1>
<h3>Ten independent Research Institutes throughout Europe have founded the EUROFRAME network, an initiative for improved forecasting and macroeconomic analysis in the European Union.</h3>
<p class="bodytext"><a href="http://www.cpb.nl/" target="_blank">CPB</a> (Netherlands), <a href="http://www.diw.de/" target="_blank">DIW Berlin</a> (Germany), <a href="http://www.esri.ie/" target="_blank">ESRI</a> (Ireland), <a href="http://www.etla.fi/" target="_blank">ETLA</a> (Finland), <a href="http://www.uni-kiel.de/ifw/" target="_blank">The Kiel Institute for the World Economy</a> (Germany), <a href="http://www.niesr.ac.uk/" target="_blank">NIESR</a> (United Kingdom), <a href="http://www.ofce.sciences-po.fr/" target="_blank">OFCE</a> (France), <a href="http://www.prometeia.it/" target="_blank">PROMETEIA</a> (Italy), <a href="http://www.wifo.ac.at/" target="_blank">WIFO</a> (Austria) and <a class="external-link-new-window" title="Opens external link in new window" href="http://www.case.com.pl/" target="_blank">CASE</a> (Poland) combine their knowledge in providing quantitative analysis, forecasts and policy recommendations for the European Union and for national decision <a title="Sitemap" data-lightbox="" href="sitemap.html">makers</a>.</p>
