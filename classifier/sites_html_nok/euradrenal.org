    <h3>EU Participation:</h3>
    <p> Euradrenal - Pathophysiology and natural course of autoimmune adrenal failure in Europe &ndash; is a project in the seventh framework programme &ldquo;Health&rdquo; with grants from the European Commission. The Health theme is a major theme of the Cooperation programme and the EU has earmarked a total of &euro; 6.1 billion for funding this theme over the duration of FP7. </p>
    <p> Euradrenal is a collaborative project, small-medium sized focused on research actions. The European Commission will finance up to 75% of the total costs of the project, while the partner-institutions are financing 25% of the project. Euradrenal has 12 partners from 7 different countries in Europe. The work planning the project started during the autumn 2006 and started 01 April 2008. Euradrenal will have a project period of 4 years, and is planned to end in March 2012. </p>
