<a name="1____966___-1"></a><h2 class="nn_sliders_title">30th September 2013 - OPENING EVENT "Horizont 2020", Düsseldorf</h2>
<p><strong>WorkAble on exhibition in the frame of the Opening Event of <a href="http://www.wissenschaft.nrw.de/forschung/foerderung/europaeische-forschungs-und-innovationsfoerderung/auftaktveranstaltung-horizont-2020/1998/" target="_blank" title="Horizont 2020">"HORIZON 2020" - The new EU framework programme</a>.</strong></p>
<p>The Ministry of Innovation, Science and Research of the State North Rhine-Westphalia invited Universities, Research Institutes and Companies to the Opening Event of HORIZON 2020 on 30th September 2013 in Düsseldorf. The opening event went along with an exhibition of successful research projects of the previous framework programme FP 7. More than 1000 guests from Science and Economy visited the event at Düsseldorfs' Rheinterrasse.</p>
<p><img src="/images/stories/fotos/horizont3-480.jpg" border="0" width="480" height="288" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" /></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">The Minister for Science of the state of North Rhine-Westphalia, Svenja Schulze (right), in discussion with Dr. Alkje Sommerfeld (left), Bielefeld University, about the EU collaborative project WorkAble. The project focusses on the difficulties young Europeans are exposed to on their way from education to work and demands a radical change of the currently insufficient institutional and political attempts to tackle these issues.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://www.wissenschaft.nrw.de/forschung/foerderung/europaeische-forschungs-und-innovationsfoerderung/auftaktveranstaltung-horizont-2020/" target="_blank">http://www.wissenschaft.nrw.de/forschung/foerderung/europaeische-forschungs-und-innovationsfoerderung/auftaktveranstaltung-horizont-2020/</a></p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Dr. Alkje Sommerfeld presenting WorkAble's DVDs - short films showing the situation of young people from nine different European countries and institutional approaches how to tackle the issue of transition from school to work.</p>
