        <div class="" id="parent-fieldname-text">
            
<img src="intro.jpg" alt="welcome to Fireshield" /> <img class="image-right" src="circular-one.jpg" alt="" /> 

<p>Fire resistant cabling is an integral part of fire protection
systems such as fire alarms and emergency lighting. Due to increased
public concern regarding fire safety and increased legislation, the
specification for fire resistant cables is becoming much more
demanding.</p>

<p>Mineral Insulated Copper Clad Cables (MICC) are currently the
highest fire resistant cabling solution available, but are very
expensive and difficult to install and integrate with other
components.</p>

<p>Soft-skinned fire resistant cables are also presently used however
they are not suitable for all applications.</p>

<img src="the-objective.jpg" alt="The Objective" /> <img class="image-right" src="circular-two.jpg" alt="" /> 

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>The main focus of the project has been the production of fire
resistant filled silicone compounds having superior char strength to
avoid crack formation during the BS EN 50200:2006 standard test, with
the advanced feature of the BS 8434-2:2003 test.</p>

<p>Further research work will continue at l'Ecole des Mines d'Alès in
France to optimise the proportion of the fillers to give the lowest
volume variation with the highest maximum compression force.<br />
<br />
These developments will make a significant contribution to fire safety
across the EU by enabling safety system manufacturers and installers to
be able to access this cutting-edge cable technology.</p>
